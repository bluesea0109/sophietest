Mount Kinabualu Climb

Sabah, Malaysia
2 - 3 days
Beginners to Advanced
US$356 - $650
A climb up the famd UNESCO World Heritage site of Mt Kinabalu takes you through several dramatically different landscapes over the course o 24 hours. Dense Borneo rainforest gives way to an enchanted bonsai-like forest, to zen-rock garden and finally to the dramatic moonscape at the summit where it feels like you are walking on the surface of the moon.
Best time to visit:

October / March. Avoid monsoon season from November - February.

3 DAY REGULAR DESCENT

Suitable for all levels of fitness
This is the regular climb up Mt Kinabalu, comprising a 8.5km trek (one way) to rach the summit at 4095m. This allows you an extra day in Kinabalu Park so that you can take your time for the climb.

HIGHTLIGHTS

  • Additional night's accommodation on the first day at Kinabalu Park HQ, less rushed morning
  • Breathtaking sunrise at the summit
  • Trek 20km over 2 days
